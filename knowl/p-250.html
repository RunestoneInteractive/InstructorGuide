If you want to grade your peer instruction assignment you must set up your questions correctly.
  • You must set the Auto-Grade column to either Peer or Peer+Chat. Setting this column to Peer will give the student credit if they have answered both vote1 and vote2. Setting it to Peer+Chat will give them credit if they have voted for vote1, interacted with their parter(s) through chat, and voted in vote2.
  • The Which to grade field must be set to All Answers This is because peer assignment grading is unique in that the student is getting credit based on multiple interactions with a question not just a single check for correctness.
