Item 4.

Your students can register for your course by using the unique name that you used when you created your course when they register at https://runestone.academy/runestone/default/user/register 4 .. If they already have a login on Runestone Academy, they can simply add another course using the new course name by going to https://runestone.academy/runestone/default/courses 5 . and then clicking on “Add Another Course”.
